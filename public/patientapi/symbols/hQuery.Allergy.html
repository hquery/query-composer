{CodedValue} product()
Food and substance allergies use the Unique Ingredient Identifier(UNII) from the FDA http://www.fda.gov/ForIndustry/DataStandards/StructuredProductLabeling/ucm162523.htm Allegies to a class of medication Shall contain a value descending from the NDF-RT concept types of Mechanism of Action - N0000000223, Physiologic Effect - N0000009802 or Chemical Structure - N0000000002. NUI will be used as the concept code. For more information, please see the Web Site http://www.cancer.gov/cancertopics/terminologyresources/page5 Allergies to a specific medication shall use RxNorm for the values.
